IEEE, an association dedicatd to advancing innovation and technologial excellence for the benefit of humanity, is the world's largest technical professional society. It is designed to serve proessionals involved in all aspets if the electrical, electronic, and computing fields and related areas of science and technology that underline modern civilization.

IEEE Region 10

The IEEE Region 10, also sometimes referred as the Asia Pacific Region, comprises of 57 sections ,6 councils, 17 Sub-sections, 515 chapters, 60 Affinity Groups and 958 Student Branches. It covers a geographical area stretching from South Korea and Japan in north-east to New Zealand in the south, and Pakistan in the west.With over 100,000 members it is one of the largest regions in IEEE.




IEEE Madras Section

The IEEE Madras Section oversees technical of IEEE bodies in the province of Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1973 as a part of the Bangalore Section, it attained Section status in 1978. The section strives on curating interactions and technical discussions among its members With various conferences annually. The Madras Section is set to complete 30 years in 2018.
